Risk, First

Sized to survive the bad days.

Conviction is worthless without the discipline to stay in the game. Four rules sit above every single position.

10%Volatility targetEvery position is scaled so the whole book aims for a steady ~10% annual swing — win or lose, the ride stays legible.
−8%Tail gateIf a stress test says a shock could push losses past −8%, the book de-levers automatically — before the storm, not after.
6×Leverage ceilingA hard cap on total exposure. No signal, however loud, can push the book past it.
8Desks of diversificationEight independent engines mean no single call decides the outcome — the winners are meant to carry the losers.

Built Like an Institution

The rules that don't bend.

Process is the edge. Four principles sit underneath everything above — the same discipline a serious desk lives by.

Tradeable P&L only

No strategy trades on a number you couldn't actually have realised. Smoothed marks and stale spreads are inputs — never returns.

The walk-forward is the judge

In-sample results don't count. Only an edge that survives out-of-sample — and keeps getting re-selected, year after year — earns capital.

We kill more than we keep

Thirty-one ideas sit in a machine-validated graveyard. A fading edge is quietly defunded, not defended.

Everything is on the record

Every version of the book is pre-registered and attested before it ships. Nothing gets silently re-tuned to flatter the numbers.
